We undertake complex construction projects in gas infrastructure in any location across India

We specialise in gas infrastructure construction and commissioning based on the requirements of our client. We are a reliable partner in carrying out construction across complex sites while adhering to the environmental standards and ensuring that the specifications of the client have been met. We follow a project centred execution approach in which we believe in consistent work processes and timely interventions ensure that the construction is carried out within the constraints of time and budget. Moreover, we also focus extensively on risk management and adopt leading practices of risk management at our construction sites. Due to our broad, hands-on experience in construction of sites for layout of gas infrastructure, we are well positioned to partner with the clients to ensure gas infrastructure connectivity across India. As a responsible employer, we employ local people and engage with local suppliers to ensure sustainability of our activities and ensure that we contribute to the job creation in the areas where the suction is carried out. In this way, we can leave a positive legacy while also bringing a reputation to our clients where the projects have been completed.

Our key service portfolio includes the following :

  • Site Mobilisation
  • Site Fabrication
  • Site Survey
  • Construction and Installation
  • Construction Management Services
