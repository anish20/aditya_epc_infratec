Client : Central U.P. Gas Limited (CUGL)

Project Name : City Gas Distribution Project in Unnao and Kanpur

Location : Unnao and Kanpur

Scope of Project :

  • Plan and prepare a schedule for execution and work implementation as per QA/QC plans to be issued by Owner / Ownerâ€™s representative.
  • Preparing route survey drawing marked for proposed gas pipeline laying
  • Co-ordination /Liaison from respective land owning agencies such as CPWD, ADA/LDA, NHAI, Nagar Nigam and any other land owing agencies for road cutting for laying of the pipelines
  • Obtaining clearances and coordination with concerned RWA of the allotted area for internal network laying.
  • Proper storing, stacking, providing security, insurance cover during storage, laying, commissioning and handing over the pipelines to Owner
  • Making trial pits to determine the underground utilities/services such as existing pipelines, Cables (Electrical/Communication), Conduits, U/G drainage, Sewers, tunnels, Subways foundations etc. for deciding optimum feasible route and depths for laying the pipelines based on the route plans
  • Installation of Safety/warning Signs and barricading of the entire route during construction
  • Supply & Installation of pipe fittings like elbow, tees, reducers, couplers, tapping saddles, transition fittings, valves etc., including construction of supports, valves pits, inspection chambers etc. as per specification
  • Laying pipelines by any methodology including trenchless technology methods with or without casing pipes (HDPE pipes) as per specifications and as directed
  • Fabrication, Supply and Inspection of good quality of GI sleeve and half concrete sleeves and other materials, fittings
  • Carrying out pneumatic testing and purging as per specifications and approved procedures
  • Supply, fabrication & Installation of RCC route marker, Pole marker with foundations, Plate markers, valve chamber etc.
  • Commissioning of gas in the tested PE Line
  • Providing skilled labour, tools, material and equipment necessary for the proper execution of task
  • Planning, detailing the size, underground utilities, foreign pipelines, crossings, and location of valve chamber, FRS / DRS / MRS as well as service line location
